Questions

Swarm FAQ

Is it actually a honey bee swarm?

Send a photo if you can. Honey bees cluster in a calm mass; wasps and hornets build paper nests and are handled differently.

Will it cost me anything?

An accessible honey bee swarm is often a free collection. We'll tell you upfront.

There are bees inside my wall &mdash; can you help?

That's an established colony, not a loose swarm, and it needs licensed removal or treatment. Our sister company handles that — call or text 717-379-3248.

How fast should I call?

Quickly — swarms often move on within a day or two, so the sooner we hear from you the better.
