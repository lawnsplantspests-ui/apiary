What Hummelstown properties typically call us about

Hummelstown is between Hershey and Harrisburg with a mix of historic borough lots, South Hanover Township subdivisions, and rural-edge properties along the Swatara Creek. The typical bee-related calls we get from the area:

  • Honey bee swarms during swarm season — clusters often on residential trees
  • Established colonies in older Hummelstown borough soffits and porch ceilings
  • Bumble bee nests on properties with mulch beds and lawn edges
  • Yellow jacket / hornet ID calls in late summer (sister LPP)

Why call a beekeeper

We're not exterminators

When you call a pest control company about bees, the technician's job is to make the bees go away. When you call The Alleman Apiary, our job is to make the bees go home with us. We use bee vacuums that collect honey bees alive, save and reuse comb where possible, and the colony goes into one of our hives — not a trash bag.

If what you actually have is yellow jackets, hornets, or wasps, our sister business Lawns Plants & Pests LLC handles those at the same phone number, 717-379-3248. One call, two specialties, all stinging insects handled the right way.
