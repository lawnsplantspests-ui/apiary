The stay

A modern barndominium, entirely yours

The Honey House is a private, modern barndominium — one bedroom, one bed, one private bath, the entire home reserved just for your group. It sits in a tucked-away neighborhood just outside the bustle of Harrisburg, on the same property as our apiary and honey stand. Quiet, comfortable, and a little bit different from your usual stay: you fall asleep a stone's throw from the hives.

It's rated five stars by guests. For current rates, availability, the full amenity list, and to reserve your dates, head to the Airbnb listing.

The beekeeping experience

Suit up and meet the bees

While you're staying with us — or as a visit on its own — you can get suited up and learn about honeybees up close. You'll have the chance to go through multiple hives alongside an experienced beekeeper, seeing the queen, the brood, and the honey the way beekeepers do.

You can be as hands-on as you like, based entirely on your comfort level. Want to just watch from inside the veil? Great. Want to lift a frame of bees yourself? Even better. It's your experience — we meet you where you are.

Questions

The Honey House FAQ

How do I book?

Both the stay and the beekeeping experience are booked through Airbnb. Use the Honey House listing for overnight stays and the beekeeping experience listing for the hive session. Rates and availability are always current there.

Do I have to do the beekeeping experience if I stay?

Not at all. The Honey House is a great quiet stay on its own. The beekeeping experience is an optional add-on for guests who want it — and it can also be booked as a standalone visit without an overnight stay.

Is it safe to be around the bees?

Yes. You'll be in a proper bee suit and veil, guided the whole time by an experienced beekeeper, and you control how close you get. If you have a known bee-sting allergy, let us know before booking and we'll talk through it.

How many people can stay?

It's a one-bedroom home best suited to a couple or small group. Check the Airbnb listing for exact guest capacity and house details.

Can I buy honey while I'm there?

Absolutely. Our self-serve honey stand is right on the property, open dawn to dusk — raw local honey, comb, and beeswax products on the honor system.
